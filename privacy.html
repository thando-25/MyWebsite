Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

At InternshipHub.co.za, we respect your privacy. This policy explains how we handle (or don't handle) your information.

No Personal Data Collected

We do not collect any personal information from visitors. There are no forms, logins, sign-ups, or email subscriptions on this site. We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or any tools that store user data.

Anonymous Analytics (Optional)

We may use basic anonymous analytics (like Google Analytics) only to see total visitor numbers and popular pages. No IP addresses, locations, or personal details are collected or stored.

External Links

Our site contains links to external company websites (e.g., Nedbank, Sasol, NSFAS). Once you click these links, you leave InternshipHub.co.za and their privacy policies apply. We are not responsible for their practices.

Payments (PayFast)

Company listings are paid via PayFast (a trusted South African payment gateway). All payment data is handled securely by PayFast — we never see or store card details.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted here with the new date.
