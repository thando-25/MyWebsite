How to Write a Winning Internship CV (With Examples)

No experience? Start with your matric results (aim for 70%+). Highlight school projects, volunteer work, or part-time jobs in skills like teamwork or problem-solving. Use bullet points: "Led group project on sustainable energy, improving efficiency by 20%." Keep it 1 page, ATS-friendly (no tables/images). Example: For Nedbank IT internship, emphasize coding clubs or Python courses. Tools: Canva for templates. 80% of recruiters reject CVs over 1 page. Customize for each application to boost chances by 50%.

Common Interview Questions for 2026 Entry-Level Jobs

Prepare for "Tell me about yourself" (keep it 1 min, focus on skills). "Why this company?" (research their values, e.g., Eskom's sustainability). "Strengths/weaknesses" (weakness = "I'm a perfectionist who learned time management"). Practice STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result). Mock interviews on YouTube. For grad programmes, expect case studies (e.g., Deloitte: "How would you advise a client on digital transformation?"). 90% of success is preparation—record yourself practicing.
