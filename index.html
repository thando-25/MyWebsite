How to Apply for 2026 Graduate Programmes in South Africa

The 2026 application season is open! Most big companies like Nedbank, Standard Bank, and Deloitte open applications between June and September. To stand out, tailor your CV to the job description, prepare for online assessments (many use Pymetrics or HireVue), and apply early — popular programmes fill fast. Research the company culture on their careers page and mention it in your motivation letter. Pro tip: Use LinkedIn to connect with current graduates in the programme — it increases your chances by 70% with personalized applications.

Top 10 Fully Funded Bursaries for 2026

Funding is key for 2026 studies. NSFAS covers full tuition for universities and TVET colleges (apply by November 2025). Funza Lushaka funds teaching degrees with R150,000/year + stipend. Sasol offers engineering bursaries (R120,000 + accommodation). Exxaro for mining (R100,000 + laptop). Anglo American for various fields (R90,000). ISFAP helps the "missing middle" (R80,000). Vodacom for IT (R70,000 + internship). Apply early — most close in August. Have your ID, matric results, and motivation letter ready.

How to Write a Winning CV with No Experience

No experience? No problem. Start with your matric results (aim for 70%+ in relevant subjects). Highlight school projects, volunteer work, or part-time jobs. Use bullet points: "Led group project on sustainable energy, improving efficiency by 20%." Keep it 1 page, ATS-friendly (no tables/images). For Nedbank IT internship, emphasize coding clubs or Python courses. Tools like Canva have free templates. 80% of recruiters reject CVs over 1 page — customize for each application.

Common Interview Questions for 2026 Entry-Level Jobs

Prepare for "Tell me about yourself" (keep it 1 minute, focus on skills). "Why this company?" (mention their values, e.g., Eskom's sustainability). "Strengths/weaknesses" (weakness = "I'm a perfectionist but learned time management"). Use STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result) for behavioral questions. Practice on YouTube mock interviews. For grad programmes, expect case studies (e.g., Deloitte: "How would you advise a client on digital transformation?"). 90% of success is preparation.

YES Programme 2026: Everything You Need to Know

The Youth Employment Service (YES) helps 100,000+ youth gain work experience. Eligibility: Unemployed, 18–35, South African citizen. Partner companies (DHL, Bidvest) offer 12-month placements with stipend R3,500–R5,000/month. Apply via yes4youth.co.za — upload CV and ID. Highlight soft skills like communication. Success stories: 70% get permanent jobs after. Check our listings for current partners.
