About InternshipHub.co.za

InternshipHub.co.za was launched in 2025 to help South African students and graduates find the best 2026 opportunities — internships, graduate programmes, bursaries, and learnerships.

We know how hard it is to search dozens of company websites, so we collect and update everything in one place — completely free.

Our mission is to give every young South African a fair chance at launching their career. We also share free guides on CV writing, interviews, and applications to help you succeed.

Founded by Tshiamo Zondo — passionate about youth employment and education in South Africa.
