About InternshipHub.co.za

InternshipHub.co.za is a completely free platform launched in 2025 to help South African students and recent graduates discover the best 2026 internships, graduate programmes, bursaries, learnerships, and YES opportunities.

We manually collect and verify opportunities from top companies like Nedbank, Standard Bank, Deloitte, Sasol, Eskom, and government departments — all in one easy place. No more searching dozens of websites or missing deadlines.

Our mission is simple: give every young South African a fair chance to launch their career. We provide not just listings, but free guides on CV writing, interview preparation, and application tips to help you stand out.

Founded by Tshiamo Zondo — a passionate advocate for youth employment in South Africa.

We are not a recruitment agency and never charge students. All applications go directly to the companies. Companies can list opportunities via our Post a Job page.
