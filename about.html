About Us

2025 Internships SA is a free platform created to help South African students and recent graduates discover the latest internships, graduate programmes, bursaries, and YES opportunities for 2025 and 2026.

We manually collect and update opportunities from trusted sources so you don’t have to search dozens of company websites. Our goal is to make launching your career easier and faster.

We are not a recruitment agency and we do not charge any fees. All applications go directly to the companies listed.

If you have any questions or find a broken link, feel free to contact us.
